Clubs

The Bay Robotics Charity supports robotics teams and clubs that align with its vision and mission. This page introduces teams that are supported and further information if you want to follow up.


FTC Team 10829
Bay Robotics

We are a group of high school students who are passionate about robotics and technology. Our mission is to inspire the next generation of engineers and innovators through hands-on learning and mentorship.

We placed #3 in the World Championship in Houston, Texas, April 17-20, 2024. See our interview with TMJ4 (local TV station) here.

This was the 3rd time Team 10829 got invited to worlds in it's 9 year history, this time as the winning alliance captain. As a rookie team they went in 2015-2016 to St. Louis and in 2019-2020 it was canceled because of COVID.
