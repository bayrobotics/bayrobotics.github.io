Financial Information

Understanding Our Financial Structure and Support Systems

Income Sources

Student Fees

Primary funding source for basic program operations:

  • Annual membership fees
  • Competition participation fees
  • Material and equipment usage fees

Grants

  • Department of Education grants
  • Corporate sponsorships
  • STEM education initiatives
  • Local business support

Program Expenses

Equipment & Materials

  • Robot components and materials
  • Tools and equipment
  • Software licenses
  • Practice field materials

Competition Costs

  • Team registration fees
  • Competition entry fees
  • Coach travel and accommodation
  • Equipment transportation

Travel & Competition Support

For student and family travel expenses to competitions, we maintain separate donation funds designated for specific events. These funds help ensure all team members can participate regardless of financial circumstances.

Competition-Specific Donations

  • Designated competition travel funds
  • Student accommodation support
  • Team transportation assistance
  • Meal subsidies during events

Coach Support

  • Travel reimbursement
  • Accommodation coverage
  • Professional development
  • Training program expenses

Financial Transparency

As a registered 501(c)(3) charity, we maintain complete transparency in our financial operations. Detailed financial reports are available to stakeholders, and we regularly update our community on the allocation of funds and program costs.
