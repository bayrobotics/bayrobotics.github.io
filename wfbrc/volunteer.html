Volunteer-Driven Excellence

The Whitefish Bay Robotics Club operates entirely through the dedication and commitment of volunteers. Every year, we rely on parents, professionals, and community members to cover the various operational needs that arise throughout our programs. Your time and expertise are essential to our success and the development of our students.

We have diverse volunteer needs that match various skill sets and interests. Whether you can commit to a few hours or ongoing support, your contribution makes a significant impact.

Help secure funding and partnerships to sustain our programs.

  • Grant writing and applications
  • Sponsorship development
  • Marketing and communications
  • Financial planning and budgeting

Technical Coaching

Share your engineering and technical expertise with students.

  • Robot design and construction guidance
  • Programming and software development
  • Mechanical engineering support
  • Electrical systems and wiring
  • CAD and 3D modeling assistance

  • Mentoring and guidance
