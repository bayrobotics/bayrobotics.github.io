Whitefish Bay Robotics Club

Empowering the Next Generation of Robotics Innovators

Our Vision

In partnership with Bay Robotics 501(c)(3), we're dedicated to fostering STEM education through innovative robotics programs that inspire, educate, and empower K-12 students.

Our Teams

Prepare students for success in FIRST Tech Challenge robotics competitions through hands-on training and mentorship.

Our Volunteers

Our club is sustained entirely by dedicated volunteers. We need parents and professionals to support various operational needs throughout the year (see the task resource document).

Onboarding & Club Operations

We collaborate with local schools to establish sustainable robotics programs that integrate with existing STEM curricula.

Our Finances

Student fees, grants, and donations are used to cover operational costs and provide a safe and engaging learning environment.
