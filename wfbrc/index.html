Whitefish Bay Robotics Club

Empowering the Next Generation of Robotics Innovators

Our Vision

In partnership with Bay Robotics 501(c)(3), we're dedicated to fostering STEM education through innovative robotics programs that inspire, educate, and empower K-12 students.

Competition Ready Teams

Prepare students for success in robotics competitions through hands-on training and mentorship.

Volunteer Professionals

Our club is sustained entirely by dedicated volunteers. We need parents and professionals to support various operational needs throughout the year.

Comprehensive Education

Collaborate with local schools to establish sustainable robotics programs that integrate with existing STEM curricula.

Industry Connections

Partner with local companies and colleges to provide real-world experience through entrepreneurial projects.

Innovation Protection

Teach students about intellectual property rights and guide them through the patent and trademark process.

Ethical Leadership

Develop understanding of ethical principles in robotics through interactive scenarios and policy development.

Community Impact

Foster a supportive environment that promotes STEM education and creates lasting positive change in our community.
